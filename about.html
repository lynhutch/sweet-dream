About Me


Hi! I’m Jocelyn. Books have always been a major part of my life since I was little. Both of my parents were avid readers of two very different genres. Whether fortunately or unfortunately, I’ve developed my dad’s love for the fantasy genre. Fantasy has always been my favorite and what I naturally gravitate to. However, as I’ve gotten older, I’ve grown a fondness for a good fluffy romance. If I’m not reading in my spare time, I’m usually crocheting or playing a video game.

About the Site


Sweet Dream Reads is my way of connecting with other readers through my own site. Sure there are plenty of other methods—and I do use them—but something about having a space that is entirely my own is unbeatable. Here, I can share and compile all my thoughts, my anticipated upcoming releases, and my ever-growing TBR (some of it physical, some of it I’m painstakingly withstanding the urge to make physical).
